Suggested future figure:

1. User enters ARG names or uploads ARG abundance CSV.
2. App normalizes ARG names.
3. App matches ARGs to the private server-side score table.
4. App returns matched ARG scores and unmatched ARG warnings.
5. App calculates gene-level contributions.
6. App sums contributions to sample-level abundance-informed index values.
7. User downloads only their own results.

The full score table remains private and is not exposed as a public download.
